An HTML game created by Colton Burdick as his CIS3304 (Advanced Web Design) final project.

The goal of the game is to simply take good care of your guests.
You win when you hold a full hotel for a whole day.
You lose if 3 guests leave or your last guest leaves.

Special guests will not appear on Quiet Mode, but guests will remind you to empty their trash and change their bedheets.
Warning: Minimizing the window will slow down the game considerably.
Leaving the game running in the background is fine (and encouraged), though.
